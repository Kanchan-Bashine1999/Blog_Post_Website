Beauty of Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand provides a wide variety of experiences that can only be characterised as an ode to the Himalayas, from the meditative vibrations of Rishikesh to the snow-covered slopes of Auli, from the beautiful carpet of the Valley of Flowers to the peaceful meadows of Mukteshwar.

Places To Visit

Almora

Almora In the Kumaon region, the town of Almora is a crown jewel offering breathtaking panoramic Himalayas views. This destination is a perfect mix of natural beauty and cultural richness, making it a standout spot in Uttarakhand. Your visit is further enriched by its bustling markets and intricately designed temples. The town is famous for handicrafts, so don’t miss out on shopping for exquisite Kumaoni artwork. The local cuisine, especially the sweets, is another highlight.

Binsar

is a dream come true for wildlife enthusiasts and solitude seekers. This secluded hill station is surrounded by the Binsar Wildlife Sanctuary, a haven for diverse flora and fauna. The sanctuary is one of the most scenic places in Uttarakhand, offering panoramic views of the majestic Himalayan peaks like Nanda Devi, Kedarnath, and Trishul.

Dehradun and Mussoorie

Ready for another top place to visit in this guide? Dehradun, the capital city, and Mussoorie, the Queen of Hills, are like two sides of a coin, each complementing the other. Dehradun is a bustling city with a blend of urban and natural charm, while Mussoorie offers a tranquil escape high up in the hills. Together, they make up some of the most beautiful places to visit in Uttarakhand.

Dhanaulti

Focused on serene landscapes and a rejuvenating atmosphere, Dhanaulti offers a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle. Located at an elevation of 2,286 meters above sea level, this tranquil spot is often considered a well-kept secret among travelers. In Uttarakhand, it stands as one of the most picturesque destinations, especially for those in search of a quiet retreat.

Nainital and Ranikhet

Famous for its iconic pear-shaped lake, Nainital and known as the Queen’s Meadow, Ranikhet offers contrasting experiences as two unique destinations. Nainital is all about the lake and the vibrant activities around it, while Ranikhet provides a tranquil escape with its lush meadows and pine forests. Together, they are among the most beautiful places to visit in Uttarakhand.

Rishikesh and Haridwar

Situated along the banks of the Ganges, Rishikesh and Haridwar provide a unique mix of spirituality and adventure. Haridwar holds the distinction of being one of India’s seven holiest cities, while Rishikesh is globally recognized as the capital of yoga. These two towns collectively rank among the top destinations to visit in Uttarakhand.
